About The Photographer

Growing up in the urban city of Dhaka from Bangladesh, I feel a unique attraction for the rural Bangladesh. Whenever I visit a rural part of the country, I have seen and felt scenes that can never be in an urban city. I hoped to capture those scences in memory through my photography.

Photography is not my profession as I am an engineer of computer Sciene. This portfolio contains my amateurish attempt to show the world as I see it.
